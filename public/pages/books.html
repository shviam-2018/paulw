Zombies Ate My Homework

School can be strange, but Swindon Academy has never seen anything like this! When the mysterious Mr. Xanar becomes the new teacher, best friends Lila and Max encounter their weirdest challenge yet. Strange worm-like creatures begin to appear, and both students and teachers start behaving oddly. Could Mr. Xanar be behind these bizarre happenings?
Join Lila and Max as they unravel a mystery that's truly out of this world in this hilarious and thrilling adventure that proves that sometimes the truth is stranger than any excuse you could dream up!
"Zombies Ate My Homework" is a hilarious and thrilling adventure for young readers aged 8-12.
