Zombies Ate My Homework

Join Lila and Max as they unravel a mystery that's truly out of this world in this hilarious and thrilling adventure that proves that sometimes the truth is stranger than any excuse you could dream up!
"Zombies Ate My Homework" is a hilarious and thrilling adventure for young readers aged 8-12.

The Awesome Adventures of Captain Doodle

Captain Doodle is not your typical pirate and this is not your typical book. Instead of a sword, he comes armed with a crayon, and the only treasure that he's looking for is a good story. And a good story is definitely to be found here, in this, the second book from the creator of Super Farty Pants! In this book, you'll join Captain Doodle, the famed book pirate, as he travels the libraries and The Seven ABC's in search of adventure, only to stumble across something he's never encountered before- an empty book, and decides to swap out all the swash-buckling for a spot of writing and doodling as he creates his own book. The Awesome Adventures of Captain Doodle is not just a book with an entertaining story (that is sure to bring a smile to both children and big kids alike) but is also a fantastic tool for encouraging your children to use their imaginations and want to create their own stories. It is perfect for reading both at home, and in the classroom, and will soon have you and your li'l swashbucklers donning paper hats and going on adventures of your own. CHILDREN'S PICTURE BOOK
